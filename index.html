Game instructions

The game consists of writing words starting with the selected letter for each category.

At the bottom is the "Start Game" button that starts the game. At first you will need to enter your username.

You will have choice of 4 categories (all categories are set in default):

  • Countries
  • Capital cities
  • Animals
  • Plants
and 3 dificulties levels:
  • easy
  • medium
  • hard

Depende of the difficulties level you will have different time for finish round.

You are going to play with computer. Depende of your and machine answers you will get different number of points..

Points system:

  • 5 points - when your word is the same as computer word.
  • 10 points - when your word is different than computer word
  • 15 points - when computer has no answer - not possible in hard difficult level

At the end of every round you will see results and achieved points.

Get a lot of points and enjoy the game!
